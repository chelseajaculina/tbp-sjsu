About Us

Academic. Excellence. Integrity.

Tau Beta Pi is the oldest national engineering honor society in the country, and one of the most nationally recognized societies. TBP stresses scholarship, personal character, and service to the profession, community, and college. We offer many student resources, including a test bank, infosessions, and resume workshops.

The California Eta Chapter at San Jos√© State University was founded in March of 1964. Today, California Eta Chapter represents the Davidson College of Engineering's top students from all engineering departments. The chapter works to provide members with a rich experience, to make available tools for students to succeed, and to do projects to improve our community.
