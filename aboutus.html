Requirements

Undergraduates

  • Juniors must be in the top 1/8 of their class.
  • Seniors must be in the top 1/5 of their class.

Graduates

  • All students must be in the top 1/5 of their class.
  • Letter of Recommendation.

Alumni

  • Must have graduated in the top 1/5 of their class.

ALL eligible candidates will be sent an invite each semester.
