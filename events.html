Pi Day

Every engineer knows about Pi Day! TBP celebrated 3.14 by having the privilege of throwing pies at the faces of some of their favorite professors. Thanks for all the support Professor Okamoto (M.E), Professor Reed (CS/SE), Professor Leinweber (BME), and Professor Wagner (ChemE)!

  • Flyer

Bent Cleaning

The officer team and fellow candidates follow tradition & cleaned the Chapter Eta Bent, the symbol of Tau Beta Pi.

Tesla Tour

Some of our members and candidates were able to attend an exclusive tour around the Tesla factory in Fremont. They were able to see how Teslas are made from start to finish!

Bowling

Love bowling? Our current TBP members, officers and candidates enjoyed a friendly game of bowling up at the bowling alley on campus in the Student Union.

Bi Annual Hike

TBP annual hike with our advisor Dr. Hsu at Sanborn County Park!

Engineering Olympics

A friendly competition that consisted of multiple fun outdoor activities!

A number of engineering clubs on campus such as American Society of Mechanical Engineergs (ASME), Students for the Exploration and Development of Space (SEDS), American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics (AIAA), and Society of Women Engineers (SWE) were all participants for this past olympics.

Spring 2019 Officer Retreat

A retreat in sunny-side San Diego during Spring Break. Our officers were able to bond, create fun memories, and enjoy the beach.
